# Description
Contains [[Information]] about identified stakeholders.

May use [[Stakeholder Engagement Assessment Matrix]].
## Includes
* **Identification information** ‚Äî Name, organizational position, location and contact details, and role on the project.
* **Assessment information** ‚Äî Major requirements, expectations, potential for influencing [[Project Outcome]]s, and the phase of the project life cycle where the stakeholder has the most influence or impact.
* **Stakeholder classification**
	* Internal/external
	* impact/influence/power/interest
	* upward/downward/outward/sideward
